Weather info and how we got it:

Weather! It's outside. It's close and personal and yet, so elusive. Our advice is based on our rudimentary knowledge of weather conditions and what we do when we experience them.

We are not experts in this regard, and if we had more time (or funding) we would hash out the details of this part of the project and make it utlra-acurate. However, this is not the case! So, our best guesses is what you get! All weather data is provided by the OpenWeatherMap API. Thanks to those folks!

UV info and how we came about it:

The sun is sooooo powerful! Which makes sunscreen very, very necessary.

All of our UV index data is provided by the OpenUV API. Thanks to those folks for all UV information and sunscreen application data based on skin type. We couldn't have made this without you!
